How it works

Shot Roulette works by looking through existing data for ingredients that work well together and recombining them into new and exciting mixed drinks!

More technically, Shot Roulette considers a graph over the known ingredients that go into mixed drinks, drawing an edge between two vertices if they are seen together in enough different drinks. It assigns to each edge a weight proportional to the number of drinks that pair appears in. When you, the user, select an ingredient to start, we look up the vertex corresponding to the drink and we take a weighted random walk three steps out.
